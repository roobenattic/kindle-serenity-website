Return & Refund Policy

At Kindle Serenity, each candle is handcrafted in small batches to ensure quality and care.

Returns

Due to the nature of our products, we do not accept returns once an order has been delivered.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your order arrives damaged or defective, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the item. We will gladly offer a replacement or refund.

Refunds

Refunds are only issued for damaged or incorrect items. Once approved, refunds will be processed to the original payment method.

Contact

For any concerns, please contact us via our Contact page.

Product Safety

Our candles are made with 100% natural coconut wax and premium, carefully selected fragrance oils.

They are free from harmful chemicals such as parabens, phthalates, and toxins.

Our products are considered safe for use around pregnant women, children, and newborns when used as directed.

However, we recommend always burning candles in a well-ventilated area and never leaving them unattended.

If you have specific sensitivities or medical concerns, please consult with your healthcare provider before use.
